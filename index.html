Caring People 2015

Problem #1: Quality

The first problem we encountered was that moms want to buy quality food for their family but it’s never easy to guarantee the quality.

Many brands are using the organic label even if the food comes from regular farms. How do you trust them?

We understand it’s the most important responsibility of moms to nurture their growing children with balanced nutrients for their physical strength and as well as intelligence.

Problem #2: Time

We realized and our data shows that working moms don’t have much time to buy food for dinner despite of the availability of healthy food at local groceries. As a result, moms feed their children with fast foods and sodas.

Kids with an unbalanced nutrition are prone to suffer obesity, diabetes, and other chronic diseases.

Problem #3: Price

You know this problem well, the cost of the organic food. We, Healthy Food, want to help moms to buy healthy food at an attractive price without losing any quality.

At Healthy Food, we want to help you get high quality and health food as easy as possible.
